About the company

Trusted partner to Canada's leading brands.

Founded in 1997, Platinum Construction Corporation has grown from a local contractor into a trusted name in commercial construction across Ontario — delivering for Starbucks, Firehouse Subs, Chipotle, Tim Hortons, Wendy's and more.

Our founder's philosophy remains at the heart of everything we do: "We are only as good as our clients." Partnering with great clients — and earning their trust through craftsmanship, transparency and delivery — is the key to our enduring success.
