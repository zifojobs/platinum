Our story

Over 27 years of building Ontario's commercial landscape.

Since its incorporation in 1997, Platinum Construction Corporation has navigated every economic cycle — and emerged stronger from each one. From our Vaughan headquarters, we now deliver projects across Ontario and beyond.

What began as a regional contractor has grown into a trusted partner to some of North America's most recognizable brands — Starbucks, Tim Hortons, Chipotle, Firehouse Subs, Wendy's, Sola Salon, Indochino, and many more.

Our growth has been built on a single principle: deliver uncompromising quality, on time, every time. That principle continues to shape every project we take on today.

Philosophy

We are only as good as our clients.

This principle, voiced by our founder, still drives every decision we make. Partnering with great clients — and earning their trust through craftsmanship, transparency and delivery — is the key to our enduring success.

01 / Craftsmanship

Quality without compromise.

Every nail, every joint, every finish — built to stand the test of time. We answer only to our own standards.

02 / Transparency

No hidden surprises.

Clear scopes, open books, honest timelines. Our clients always know where they stand — at every stage.

03 / Delivery

On time. On budget. On brand.

Our network of trusted vendors and expert managers makes turnkey delivery our signature strength.
