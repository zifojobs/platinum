A — The Opportunity

Today, when a developer, franchise group or hotel ownership team in Ontario looks for a general contractor, their search starts on Google — not in the Yellow Pages. Whether they win a project or not depends largely on whether Platinum appears in the first five results.

A.1 The current site has the content but not the structure

The legacy site at platinumconstruction.com communicates the brand well, but it was not built for search engines. There are no structured-data signals, no Open Graph metadata, no XML sitemap and no per-page descriptions tailored to keywords prospects actually type. As a result, Google treats Platinum as a generic page rather than as a 27-year-old commercial GC headquartered in Vaughan.

A.2 The new site is the right moment to fix it

Rebuilding the site is a once-in-a-decade window to bake SEO into the foundation. The cost of doing it now is marginal; the cost of retrofitting it after launch is roughly 4× higher and risks losing whatever ranking has accumulated.

BOTTOM LINE   Platinum has the credentials, the brand and the portfolio. The strategy below makes Google see that — and translate it into qualified inbound leads from commercial developers, franchise teams and hotel ownership groups across Ontario.

03 — Strategy
The five pillars.
C — What We'll Do

Each pillar is a self-contained workstream. They compound: doing all five together produces a stronger result than the sum of parts.

PILLAR 01

On-page foundations

Unique, keyword-targeted title and description on every page. Canonical URLs. Open Graph and Twitter Card metadata so links shared on LinkedIn or WhatsApp render with the brand image and headline rather than a blank rectangle.

PILLAR 02

Structured data (JSON-LD)

Schema.org markup that tells Google explicitly what Platinum is: LocalBusiness in Vaughan, GeneralContractor, with phone, address, hours, areas served. Unlocks rich snippets and Google Maps visibility.

PILLAR 03

Crawlability

XML sitemap auto-generated and declared in robots.txt. Internal linking pattern that funnels authority toward the revenue pages (Services, Gallery, Contact). Submitted to Google Search Console day one.

PILLAR 04

Local & brand SEO

Connect & optimize the Google Business Profile, align NAP (name / address / phone) across web, GBP and citations. Target high-intent local keywords: "commercial general contractor Vaughan", "franchise build-out Ontario", etc.

PILLAR 05

Performance & Core Web Vitals

Compress and lazy-load the hero and project videos, defer non-critical JS, preconnect to font CDNs. Targets: LCP < 2.5s, CLS < 0.1, INP < 200ms on a mid-tier mobile over 4G — the thresholds Google uses as a ranking signal.

After

Example 2 — Telling Google "we are a real local business"

<script type="application/ld+json"> { "@context": "https://schema.org", "@type": "GeneralContractor", "name": "Platinum Construction Corporation", "foundingDate": "1997", "address": { "streetAddress": "83 Citation Dr. Unit 2", "addressLocality": "Vaughan", "addressRegion": "ON", "postalCode": "L4K 2Z6" }, "telephone": "+1-905-763-8119", "areaServed": [ "Ontario", "Greater Toronto Area" ], "sameAs": [ "https://facebook.com/...", "https://linkedin.com/..." ] } </script>
WHY IT MATTERS   This single block is what makes Platinum show up in Google Maps, in the "knowledge panel" on the right side of brand searches, and as a result with phone + hours + reviews instead of just a blue link.

E — Roadmap

The work is scoped to ship alongside the new website launch, with a follow-up checkpoint at 90 days to measure traction.

PHASE 01 · WEEK 1

On-page foundations & structured data

Per-page titles, descriptions, canonicals, Open Graph metadata, JSON-LD blocks. Sitemap.xml + robots.txt generated and submitted to Google Search Console. Bing Webmaster Tools registered in parallel.

PHASE 02 · WEEK 2

Performance & image optimization

Hero and project videos re-encoded, lazy-loading rolled out across the gallery, alt attributes written for every project photo. Lighthouse audit pass: target 90+ on Performance / SEO / Accessibility.

PHASE 03 · WEEK 3–4

Local SEO activation

Google Business Profile claimed, photos uploaded, services and service areas configured. NAP citations checked across Yelp, Yellow Pages, BBB. First three project case-studies published as long-form pages with location keywords.

PHASE 04 · DAY 90

Measurement & iteration

Search Console review: indexed pages, impressions, click- through rate, top-ranking queries. Identify the keywords showing momentum and double down with new content. Adjust based on what real Ontario prospects are typing.

E.1 What success looks like at 90 days

  • 100% of pages indexed in Google Search Console (vs ~partial today)
  • Brand search "Platinum Construction Vaughan" returns the new site #1, with the knowledge panel populated
  • Google Maps listing live with photos, hours and direct contact
  • Top-3 ranking on at least two long-tail commercial queries (e.g. "franchise build-out contractor Ontario")
  • Lighthouse SEO score ≥ 95 on every page
